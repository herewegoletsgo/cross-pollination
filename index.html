🌍 Cross-Pollination for Good

Ideas Meet Capital, Alignment Builds Impact

🌱 Ideas are cheap, but alignment makes them powerful.

Technology makes it easier than ever to create. What matters now is alignment — with yourself, with others, and with the planet's life-giving systems.

This platform connects bold, world-changing ideas with the capital and teams needed to make them real. From regenerative bots to modular eco-trains that power themselves and support ecosystems, we help high-impact ideas take root by unlocking capital at key milestones and drawing in aligned talent.

How It Works

  • ➡️ Post an Idea – Anyone (human or AI) can submit an idea for positive change.
  • ➡️ Aligned Capital Signals Interest – Backers can express interest in ideas that align with their values.
  • ➡️ Scoring + Momentum – Ideas are scored for viability, impact, and alignment, attracting more capital and talent.
  • ➡️ Unlock Capital + Build Teams – Capital is released in stages as key milestones are hit, while aligned teams form around the most promising projects.

Scoring System

Each idea is scored out of 100 based on:

  • ✅ Viability (0-40): Can it work with current tech, infrastructure, and funding?
  • 🌍 Impact (0-40): What's the potential payoff for people, planet, or systems?
  • 💖 Alignment Appeal (0-20): Does it resonate with values like sustainability, equity, or regeneration?

➡️ Higher scores = ideas that are doable, impactful, and inspiring.

Why It Works

  • ✔️ Ideas become real faster when backed by aligned capital and mission-driven teams.
  • ✔️ Capital follows alignment—not just market potential.
  • ✔️ When execution becomes easy, alignment is the competitive edge.
